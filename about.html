                                                                                     Rules to follow

* Library users are strictly prohibited from taking the borrowed and stamped books again inside the library.  
 * Books will not be issued to members on the ID card of others.
*   If any book is lost or damaged beyond repair, the person  responsible shall replace it with a
       new copy or pay double the cost of the  recent price and handling charges or as   may be decided by the Librarian.
*   Membership Cards are not transferable.
*    Books will not be issued to students on the Membership Card of staff members.
*   Books will be issued subject to availability only.  
*  Absence from the college will not be considered as an excuse for the delay in the return of books. 
*   The Borrower will be responsible for any loss or non-return of any books issued against his / her library Card.
